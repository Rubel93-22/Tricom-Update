Power & Performance

The Apple MacBook Pro 14" M3 is equipped with the groundbreaking Apple M3 chip, featuring an 8-core CPU (4 performance + 4 efficiency cores), 10-core GPU with hardware-accelerated ray tracing, and a 16-core Neural Engine for fast machine learning tasks. With 8GB RAM and a 512GB SSD, it delivers exceptional performance for multitasking, development, and creative workloads. The 100GB/s memory bandwidth and advanced media engine enable fast rendering, encoding, and playback for high-end video editing and content creation..

Stunning Display & Graphics

Experience incredible visuals on the 14.2" Liquid Retina XDR display (3024×1964), with up to 1600 nits peak brightness, 120Hz ProMotion refresh rate, and support for HDR content. The display is factory-calibrated with wide color (P3) and True Tone for vivid, accurate color. Its advanced GPU architecture introduces Dynamic Caching and mesh shading, delivering up to 2.5x faster graphics performance—ideal for gaming, rendering, and design work.

Immersive Audio, Camera & User Experience

Enjoy a studio-like media experience with a six-speaker sound system featuring Dolby Atmos and Spatial Audio. The 1080p FaceTime HD camera ensures clear video calls, while the three-mic array captures your voice with precision and filters out background noise. The Magic Keyboard includes a full-height function row and Touch ID for fast, secure access and payments. Crafted in a sleek, space gray aluminum chassis, the MacBook Pro combines premium design with powerful functionality.
